Mission

Enable in-person interactions with students around you

Modernize your university student services

Why?

At UCSD I faced the same problems that many of you may be facing today - everything from RA's, student services, to meeting new people.

RA's have it hard - building relationships across the whole floor and planning activities to get you more involved when it's so easy to chat with high school friends or start a game of League. It's overwhelming to keep on top off all the student services available to you, it's a pain to walk into the offices when you have a quick question, and a reply to my email takes too long. Finally meeting new students is hard - its easy for some but not everyone can strike a conversation with a random person; high school doesn't prepare you for that.

My goal is to make it easier for you to meet new students and get any help you need from your university student services.

-Vinit
