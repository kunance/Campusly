How to Apply Online

1. Create your profile (online application)

  • Include your contact, address, and move-in information
  • One application works for all properties

2. Apply for a property

  • Find your property from your favorite search site (Craigslist, Lovely, etc.) or invite an owner
  • Share your desired move-in date and lease length
  • Press Apply and your application is submitted!

3. Complete credit check

  • Complete your Experian credit check
  • Your one credit report works across multiple properties
  • Enjoy all the time you just saved!
